MDH (Mahashian Di Hatti) is a renowned Indian brand of spice blends established in 1919 by Mahashay Chunnilal Gulati in Sialkot, now in Pakistan. After partition, it was reestablished in Delhi, India, and has since become a household name for its authentic, ready-to-use spice mixes tailored to Indian cuisine. MDH offers a wide variety of masalas like Garam Masala, Chana Masala, Kitchen King, and Pav Bhaji Masala, catering to both regional and traditional dishes. Known for its consistent quality and authentic flavors, MDH spices have made cooking Indian food convenient for generations. The brand's iconic colorful packaging and global reach have made it popular in over 50 countries. Mahashay Dharampal Gulati, the face of MDH, was awarded the Padma Bhushan in 2019 for his contributions to the Indian spice industry.
